GAME FEATURES

🃏
CARD COLLECTING
Collect 20+ unique pixel heroes across 5 classes — Warriors, Mages, Archers, Healers, Assassins. Each card has unique stats and skills.
⚔️
AUTO BATTLES
Build your deck, deploy your formation, and watch your heroes fight in real-time. Strategic positioning wins battles.
🔗
SYNERGY SYSTEM
Stack class combos for powerful buffs. 2 Warriors = +20% DEF. 3 Mages = +30% ATK. Cross-class combos unlock even stronger effects.
📦
CARD PACKS
Buy packs with gold or gems. Animated opening reveals your new cards. Higher rarity = better stats and rarer skills.
📈
PROGRESSION
Level up to unlock new heroes, earn gold and gems. Every 2 levels unlocks a new hero card for your collection.
🏪
MARKETPLACE
Trade cards with other players. Buy from AI sellers or list your own cards. Build the perfect deck through trading.

HOW TO PLAY

1

GET YOUR STARTER CARDS

Begin with 3 heroes — Iron Knight, Fire Mage, and Holy Priest. These form your first battle deck.

2

BUILD YOUR FORMATION

Place your heroes on a 2x3 grid. Front row takes more damage but deals more. Back row is safer for mages and healers.

3

BATTLE & EARN

Fight PvE stages to earn gold, gems, and XP. Win battles to unlock new heroes and level up your collection.

4

COLLECT & UPGRADE

Open card packs, trade on the marketplace, and build the ultimate deck. Unlock all 20+ heroes!
