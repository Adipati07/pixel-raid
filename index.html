How to Play

Get started in 4 easy steps

1

Get Your Starter Cards

Begin with 3 heroes — Iron Knight, Fire Mage, and Holy Priest. These form your first battle deck.

2

Build Your Formation

Place your heroes on a 2x3 grid. Front row takes more damage but deals more. Back row is safer.

3

Battle & Earn

Fight PvE stages to earn gold, gems, and XP. Win battles to unlock new heroes and level up.

4

Collect & Upgrade

Open card packs, trade on the marketplace, and build the ultimate deck. Unlock all 20+ heroes!
